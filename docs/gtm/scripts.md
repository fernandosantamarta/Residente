## 1. The 5-touch outbound sequence (to the registered agent / president)

> Targets come from the lead sheet (`lead-sheet-template.csv`). **Prioritize "hot" accounts** (≥2–3 owners already on the free resident tier) — open with the demand-concentration line.

**Subject:** `[Association]: the 2026 records-website law — we'll set it up free`

>

>

>

>
> [Name] · Residente · [phone] · [postal address]
> *Educational, not legal advice — for your association's specifics, consult your attorney. Reply "stop" and I won't email again.*

## 4. The 15-minute demo script (outline)
1. **(2 min) The money view** — "This is what every owner sees: spending vs. budget, reserves, where each dollar of dues goes." *(Lead here — it's the universal hook.)*
2. **(3 min) The board cockpit** — dues/collections, official-records website (the 2026 mandate, handled), meeting notices + minutes.
3. **(3 min) The deadline dashboard** — "Every FL 718/720 deadline tied to its statute, on one screen." *(General; "consult your attorney" for specifics.)*
4. **(2 min) The vote they can't rig** — secret ballot, operator-blind. "We can't read it. No competitor ships this."
5. **(2 min) The 95/5** — what we automate vs. the 5% to keep a bookkeeper/handyman for. Honesty = trust.
6. **(3 min) The close** — pricing (residents free, $29/board), free white-glove setup, founding-member lock. Book the setup date.
